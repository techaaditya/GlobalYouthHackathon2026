// FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for participants, prospective sponsors, and partners about the hackathon's tracks, judging, and logistics.

It is a free, global online hackathon organized by the Bagmati UNESCO Club that challenges young innovators, students, developers, researchers, and designers to build practical, responsible AI solutions for real-world problems. Projects are submitted online via Devpost, and the top finalist teams are invited to a live final showcase in China.
Teams build along six thematic areas: AI for Education & Digital Literacy; Climate Action & Sustainability; Digital Trust, Misinformation & Online Safety; Inclusion, Accessibility & Low-Resource Communities; Health, Wellbeing & Social Good; and AI Governance, Transparency & Responsible Data Use. Pick the track that best fits your idea.
Participation is free and open to teams from anywhere in the world — students, developers, researchers, designers, educators, and early-stage innovators are all welcome. Registration and project submission are handled through Devpost, where you'll also find the guidelines, challenge tracks, and submission requirements.
The top five finalist teams will be invited to present their projects at the live final showcase in China. Final attendance logistics, travel support, prize pool, and rewards depend on confirmed sponsorship and partnership support, and will be shared with finalists ahead of the showcase.
Judges and mentors review projects on innovation, ethical impact, technical quality, feasibility, inclusion, transparency, and real-world relevance. Each thematic track winner is recognized, and rewards may include cash prizes, cloud/AI tool credits, incubation support, certificates, and mentorship — finalized as sponsorship is confirmed.
Sponsors and partners can support the hackathon through funding, mentorship, judging, cloud/API credits, technical tools, prizes, travel support for finalists, or media and capacity-development sessions. In return you gain visibility across a global responsible-AI program and recognition in the post-event impact report. Connect through our Secretariat offices inside the Contact section.
